## Executive Summary

PowerPlaza is a Korean power supply manufacturer established in 1993, specializing in DC-DC and AC-DC converters, and EV components. The company offers approximately 700 standardized converter products across 21 DC-DC series, 8 AC-DC series, and 5 EV component categories. This catalog is designed for reseller website development.
